Worship During the COVID-19 Pandemic

All service times and opening hours are listed in Eastern Time.

In-Person Opening Hours and Service Times

For those who are want to attend services in person, and who are able to commute safely, Saint Mary's is open with limited capacity and strict safety guidelines for one Mass every day. Opening hours and services times are as follows:

Sunday

The church is open
10:00 AM to 12:30 PM
Sunday Eucharist begins at
11:00 AM

Monday - Saturday

The church is open
11:00 AM to 2:00 PM
Daily Mass begins at
12:10 PM

Safety Guidelines

Visitors must follow these guidelines at all times to ensure the health and safety of all. Thank you for your understanding and patience!

  • Masks must be worn in the church.
  • Please use hand sanitizer when you enter and leave.
  • Safe-distancing as indicated must be maintained at all times.
  • We can welcome 40 people into the church at any one time. This includes 6 couples, who may sit together.
  • Luggage and other large packages may not be brought into the church.
  • Please enter and exit only on West 46th Street.

Sunday Mass Online

We maintain our communal worship in these times with Sunday Mass streamed live on our Facebook page, beginning at 11:00 AM. Service bulletins and readings may be downloaded from our homepage. All streamed Masses are available in our archives for you to watch at any time.
