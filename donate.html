Giving to St. Mary’s

Saint Mary’s is supported by members and friends who value the mission and witness of the Episcopal parish for Times Square. Such support makes it possible for our church to remain open every day of the year and to be open to all. The regular services of the church—Morning and Evening Prayer and the Holy Eucharist—are celebrated daily. The building’s open doors and our commitment to worship, welcome, and service invite all to enter, rest, and pray in the beauty of holiness.

We invite our members, friends, and neighbors to support our mission by making an annual pledge to support the operating budget of the parish or by making recurring or one-time gifts. If you have any questions or concerns, please contact Christopher Howatt, our parish administrator. We thank you for your support of this historic parish in Times Square!
