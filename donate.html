Giving to St. Mary’s

Donate Now

You can support Saint Mary's by making a one-time or recurring donation on your credit card. The button above will open a form where you can select the amount you'd like to give, and tell us how you'd like it to be used.

show instructions hide instructions

The "Funds" select box will allow you to donate directly to our general fund, make your pledge, gift flowers for the alar, or support the Music ministry. You can use the "Memo" box to give us more details about how you'd like your gift to be used, or to dedicate your gift.

After you click the Continue button, you'll be able to enter either your credit card or bank information. All of your information is fully secured with the Abundant giving tool.

If you wish, you can use the gray slider button on the bottom left to help cover our processing costs for online donations.

Before you leave, you'll be offered the option of setting up an account. We highly recommend that you do so, especially if you're making a recurring donation. This will allow you to manage your donations going forward.

Values statement to highlight

As a member or friend of Saint Mary’s, you value the mission and witness of the Episcopal parish for Times Square. Your support makes it possible for our church to remain open every day of the year and to be open to all. Together, our open doors and our commitment to worship, welcome, and service invite all to enter, rest, and pray in the beauty of holiness.

Your gift supports our mission and ministry:

  • continuing our historial purpose of daily worship in Times Square since December 8, 1870
  • building our community beyond those who can join us in person with the live-streaming of Solemn Mass every Sunday
  • expanding Neighbors in Need, our program to provide clothing, hygeine items, footwear, and transportation cards to the unhoused members of our Times Square community
