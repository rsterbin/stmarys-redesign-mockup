Sundays

Saturday Evening Vigil Mass

This is the first "Sunday Mass." This is a said Eucharist and always includes a sermon. Like all Masses at Saint Mary's, it is celebrated at the high altar and people are invited to sit in the chancel or in the nave. The service lasts about 40 minutes.

Morning Prayer 8:30 AM

All are invited to sit in the chancel of the church with the parish clergy and sisters, near the high altar. This is the daily morning service of the Church. The service includes the recitation of psalms, readings from the Bible and prayers to begin the day. During the academic year, Morning Prayer is sung to simple plainsong chant. The service is also called "Matins." The service lasts about 20 minutes when it is said, 25 when it is sung.

Mass 9:00 AM & 10:00 AM

This is a said Eucharist and always includes a sermon. All Masses are celebrated at the high altar. Coffee is served in the parish hall following all Sunday morning Masses. The service lasts about 40 minutes.

Sunday School for Children and Adult Christian Education 10:00 AM - 10:45 AM

Sunday School for Children and Adult Christian Education classes are offered during the academic year. Sunday School for children is led by Deacon Rebecca Weiner Tompkins. Classes for adults are held in the Mission House.
