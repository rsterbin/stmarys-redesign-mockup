Worship At Saint Mary's

At Saint Mary's, our common life is shaped by our commitment to others and by worshiping in the beauty of holiness. The calendar of daily celebrations can be found here. We look forward to the resumption of our pre-pandemic schedule when circumstances permit.

All service times and opening hours are listed in Eastern Time.

Worship in Person

For those who are want to attend services in person, and who are able to commute safely, Saint Mary’s is open daily from 10:00 am until 5:30 pm, or until 2:00 pm on federal holidays.

Sunday Solemn Mass 11:00 AM

Monday through Saturday Daily Mass 12:10 PM; Daily Evening Prayer 5:00 PM

Guidelines for safe worship are listed in detail below.

Worship Online

We maintain our communal worship in these times with Solemn Mass streamed live on our Vimeo page.

Sunday Solemn Mass 11:00 AM

Service bulletins and readings may be downloaded from our homepage. All streamed Masses are available in our archives for you to watch at any time.

Guidelines for Worship and Prayer in the Church

Guidelines must be followed for the health and safety of all.

  • Masks & Face Coverings: We require all persons, including those who are fully vaccinated, to wear face coverings for visiting and attending worship.
  • Social Distancing: We request all persons, including those who are fully vaccinated, to practice safe distancing when they are with people who are not a part of their household.
  • Seating: Our ushers can assist you in choosing a pew for safe distancing.
  • Concern for Ourselves and Others In all of this, our guiding principles are being honest about one’s health and vaccination status, trusting and caring for others, and giving respect for others in our community.

Saint Mary's Still Needs Your Support

We are entering a time of transition at Saint Mary's. We are emerging from the pandemic and out from under the shroud of our 46th Street scaffolding. The restored fascade and the resumption of Solemn Mass will revitalize our traditional ministries, both at the altar and in our community, and the installation of audiovisual equipment empowers new ministries like live streaming, which can amplify Saint Mary's message and music to members and friends across the nation and globe.

To seize these opportunities, we need your help. Can you give today to make sure that our work can continue?
