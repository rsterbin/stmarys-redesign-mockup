  • If you need a COVID-19 test

    Free COVID-19 testing is available to all New Yorkers. The testing site nearest to Saint Mary's is:

    CityMD Urgent Care- West 42nd
    345 W 42nd St.
    New York, NY 10036
    (646) 518-0159
    8AM - 8PM

    To find a site near you, scroll to the bottom of the NYC COVID-19 Testing Page

  • If you need food

    If you need a meal right now

    Saint Mary's supports the Holy Apostles Soup Kitchen, located at 28th Street and 9th Avenue. The soup kitchen is open Monday-Friday, 10:30AM to 12:30 PM. Hot meals are provided to-go for everyone's safety.

    Free grab-and-go meals are available to all NYC public school students and families while the school buildings are closed. Each school provides meals from 9am to noon on weekdays. No documentation is required.

    Select NYC public schools also provide grab-and-go meals to the general public on weekdays 3:00 PM to 5:00 PM. The nearest location to Saint Mary's is:

    Beacon High School
    521 West 43rd Street
    *Halal meals available
    *This site is fully accessible

    More information about NYC schools meals

    If you need groceries

    Saint Mary's supports the Saint Clement’s Food Pantry, located on 46th Street between 9th and 10th Avenues. The pantry provides non-perishable food items organized into nutritious, balanced meals and distributed to clients each week. The pantry is open every two weeks on Friday, 2:00 PM to 6:00 PM, and Saturday, 8:30 AM to 11:30 AM. Dates are posted outside, in front of the church. For more information, contact Saint Clement's at 212-246-7277.

    In this time of crisis, NYC has gathered information on soup kitchens, food pantries, greenmarkets, and grocery stores across the city. You can use the Get Food NYC Map to find resources near you.

  • If you're struggling with substance abuse

    Saint Mary's Mission House is home to three Alcoholics Anonymous groups. Although COVID-19 has made physical meetings impossible for now, each group meets remotely at the same times:

    "Amazing Grace"
    Monday-Friday, 7:30 AM
    "New Meets West"
    Monday-Friday, 12:30 PM
    Monday-Friday, 5:30 PM
    Saturdays, 10:00 AM
    Sundays, 5:00 PM
    "Come Early, Come Often"
    Thursdays, 6:00 AM

    You can find Zoom login information by looking up the group by name on the Inter-Group Association of A.A. of New York's Remote Meeting List.
